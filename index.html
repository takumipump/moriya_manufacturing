ABOUT COMPANY

Thank you for visiting our website. Moriya Chokoku Kougei is a company founded in 1969. At our company, we focus on mass-produced products such as writing implements, smoking implements,accessories, fishing tackle, mobile phone parts, etc., but we have also been involved in prototype products that are the patterns and shapes of products, such as personalization. Recently, the sophistication of machine technology has made it possible to handle various patterns and shapes that meet the needs of the times. In 2009, our company Mamoru Moriya was recommended by the Tokyo Industrial Sculpture Cooperative Association, and after an interview and technical examination by the Tokyo Metropolitan Government, he was awarded the Tokyo Meister. We will never be satisfied with the current technology, and all of our employees will do their best to take on new challenges. Please feel free to contact us by phone, fax, or e-mail if you have any questions about sculpture. Thank you for your continued support of Moriya Chokoku Kogei.
