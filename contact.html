dia cut

A dedicated diamond-cutting machine, miniaturized from a milling machine, is used for cutting various manufacturers' logos and marks with a diamond tool, providing a glossy cutting process.
We perform the machining of logos and marks on curved surfaces using contouring equipment and R-cut. This ensures that the machining is tailored to the curvature of the surface. Additionally, we handle other processes such as straight sections, blind holes, C-face diamond cutting, and more, all customized to meet the specific needs of our customers

Shaper cutting Machine

We specialize in less complex machining than NC processing, mainly focusing on flat surface machining and radial machining.
Our expertise lies in producing aesthetically pleasing products with even pitch patterns, and we strive to meet our customers' needs

NC Machine

We also cater to mass production capabilities through multi-axis machining. Moreover, we can accommodate small-batch production for mock-ups, prototype molds, and more, leveraging one of the significant advantages of NC engraving machines

General machine tools

We create various jigs, folders, and prototypes in-house, designing handles and shapes

Laser cutting and engraving

We use cutting-edge laser marker (YV04) capable of ultra-fast printing with high-density dots and exquisite finishing, even for extremely small logos and marks. We cater to a wide range of needs, from QR codes to barcodes, photo images, and even grounding by laser.
